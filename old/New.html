About Me

I turn complexity into decisions people trust.

Trained in Computer Science, I crossed over to design 8+ years ago because I cared more about the people on the other side of the screen than the syntax behind it. Since then I've shipped 47 products across B2B SaaS, healthcare, fintech, manufacturing, AgriTech and enterprise, most of them living somewhere between data complexity and decision urgency.

My current focus is the seam where AI meets product design: LLM agents, ambient assistance, and design systems that can reason. I integrate AI into the workflow without losing the human craft: clear hierarchy, honest feedback, and interfaces that let teams act, not just look at numbers.
