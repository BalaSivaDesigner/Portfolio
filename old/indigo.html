01 The Challenge

Tarmac blindness
was a safety risk.

IndiGo operates 20+ ground vehicles per airport on the tarmac — ambulances, fire trucks, fuel dispensers, sweepers, passenger coaches. Before this system, coordination was entirely radio-based. No one could see the full picture. Near-misses were rising. Emergency response times were inconsistent and unmeasured.

⚠
No real-time location data Vehicle positions were unknown unless radioed in. Emergency vehicles took minutes to locate during critical incidents.
⛔
Zero geofencing enforcement Restricted tarmac zones were managed by human spotters. Unauthorized access went undetected until an incident occurred.
📋
Fragmented vehicle health data Maintenance logs were paper-based. Low-fuel and equipment failures were discovered reactively, not proactively.
02 Design Strategy

Three decisions that shaped everything.

Ground teams don't need dashboards — they need situational clarity. Every design choice had to pass one test: does this help someone act faster, or just inform them?

01
Make the invisible visible

A live map with all 58 vehicles moving in real time. Not a list, not a table — a spatial, instant understanding of the tarmac. Asset positions update every 3 seconds.

02
Priority over information

Three alert tiers — Critical, Warning, Info — with critical alerts surfaced as a persistent overlay. The team sees the most urgent issue first, not the most recent one.

03
Prevent before you respond

Geofencing shifts safety from human vigilance to system enforcement. Draw a zone, assign rules, and the platform auto-generates violation alerts the moment a boundary is breached.

Core Insight

Ground teams don't need more data — they need to know three things instantly: Is the tarmac safe? Where is every vehicle? What needs action right now?

03 The Solution

One system. Every airport. All vehicles.

Four integrated modules, each answering a single operational question — designed so a ground controller can orient, act, and hand off without context-switching.

Live Map Dashboard

All 58 assets across 3 airports updating every 3 seconds. Click any vehicle for full details — fuel level, speed, heading, maintenance status, and active violations.

🛡
Geofence Safety Zones

Draw any polygon on the tarmac. Assign it as a Red Zone, Safe Corridor, or Maintenance Bay. The system auto-triggers alerts the moment a vehicle breaches the boundary.

⚡
Alert Hierarchy

Critical alerts surface as a persistent on-screen overlay — never missed. Warning and info tiers are accessible in the Fleet Health panel. One tap zooms the map directly to the vehicle.
